# R21.13 — Subtask due dates never flagged overdue

**Status:** done (merged in `a16e078`)
**Files:** `src/components/sortable-deliverables.tsx`, `src/app/(app)/my-tasks/page.tsx`

## Spec

**Problem:** Deliverables (project page) and action items (dashboard + My Tasks) render overdue dates red,
but subtasks — the granular unit a lead delegates — were never flagged past-due on any surface, hampering a
lead's ability to spot slipping work.

**Approach:** On the project detail page (`sortable-deliverables.tsx`), a `subtaskOverdue` flag renders the
due date red with an "Overdue" tooltip when a still-open subtask is past due, mirroring the deliverable
treatment. On My Tasks, subtask due dates turn red when overdue, matching the action-item styling directly
above them. Uses the app-wide convention — plain `new Date(date) < new Date()` (due-today counts as
overdue) — rather than a stricter same-day check.
